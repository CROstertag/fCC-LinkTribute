Link, the Hero of Hyrule

Twenty adventures later, this Hylian keeps saving Hyrule again and again.

Here is a list of Link's accomplishments:

  • Found and recovered the Triforce of Wisdom
  • Found and recovered the Triforce of Courage
  • Traveled back and forth in time an unintelligible number of times in the current timeline
  • Recovered the Master Sword at least 11 times
  • Defeated Ganon over 9 times
  • Saved Princess Zelda every time she needed saving
  • Discovered hundreds and hundreds of Koroks
  • Never harmed a chicken
  • Never gotten lost in the Lost Woods
  • Current Hyrule shield-surfing distance record holder
